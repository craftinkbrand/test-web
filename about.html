OUR HISTORY

DIMOS Furniture is one of the leading and most trusted furniture vendors in Kerala, well known for their alluring and inimitably designed furniture’s. They trade more than 50 top brands along with their ethnic furniture’s manufactured by themselves that showcase the unique talent and dedication.

DIMOS was established in 1994 at Malappuram, within a short span of 27 years, we were able to emerge as first choice of our proud customers with our never ceasing commitment to manufacture quality customized furnitures. Our furniture showroom in Kollam is one of the largest furniture shops in Kerala and has grown to become a household name especially in Kerala. We have several branches across Kerala. From Pazhayatinkuzhi, Calicut, Pallimukku, Chanthanathoppu, Kondotty, Kottarakkara and Karunagappalli.
